- Ingredients:
  * 1oz [[Cynar]]
  * .5oz [[Punt e Mes]]
  * .5oz [[[[lemon]] juice]]
  * 2 dashes [[grapefruit [[bitters]]]]
  * [[American [[IPA]]]]
- Directions:
  * Combine all ingredients except for the beer in a cocktail shaker and shake.
  * Strain into [[punch mug]] or [[double rocks glass]].
  * Top (VERY GENTLY) with beer.
