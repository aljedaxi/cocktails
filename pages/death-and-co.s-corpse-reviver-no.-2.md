## Description
## Ingredients
- 3/4 ounce gin, preferably Beefeater
- 3/4 ounce Cointreau
- 3/4 ounce Cocchi Americano
- 3/4 ounce lemon juice
- 2 dashes absinthe
## Instructions
- Combine all ingredients in a mixing tin and shake with ice.
- Strain into a chilled coupe or cocktail glass.
