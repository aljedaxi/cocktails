- Description
  The Hanky Panky dates back to the early 1900s, when it was invented at Londonâ€™s Savoy Hotel. It combines gin, sweet vermouth and Fernet-Branca
- Ingredients
	- 1 1/2 ounces [[gin]]
	- 1 1/2 ounces [[sweet [[vermouth]]]]
	- 2 dashes [[[[Fernet]]-Branca]]
	- Garnish: orange twist
- Steps
	- Add the gin, sweet vermouth and Fernet-Branca into a mixing glass with ice and stir until well-chilled.
	- Strain into a chilled cocktail glass.
	- Garnish with an orange twist.
