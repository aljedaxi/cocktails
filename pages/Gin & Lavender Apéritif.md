- Ingredients
	- 1.5 Oz. [[gin]]
	- .75 Oz. [[Cocchi Americano]]
	- .75 Oz. [[[[lemon]] juice]]
	- .75 Oz. [[[[lavender]] [[syrup]]]]
	- [[Lavender]] sprigs to garnish
- Instructions
	- Combine all of your ingredients in a cocktail shaker.
	- Add ice!
	- [[shake]] vigorously.
	- Strain into a [[coupe]] glass.
	- Garnish with fresh or dried lavender sprigs.
