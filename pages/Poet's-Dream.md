## Description
A wacky [[martini]]
## Ingredients
- 2 oz [[gin]] 
- .25 oz [[Bénédictine]] D.O.M. liqueur
- .75 oz [[dry vermouth]] 
- 2 [[orange bitters]]
## Steps
- STIR all ingredients with ice and strain into chilled glass
