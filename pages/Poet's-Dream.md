## Description
Subtly boozy, honeyed and herbal.
## Ingredients
- 30 ml [[gin]] 
- 15 ml [[Bénédictine]] D.O.M. liqueur
- 30 ml [[dry vermouth]] 
- 2 [[orange bitters]] 
- 10 ml Chilled water (omit if using wet ice)
## Steps
- STIR all ingredients with ice and strain into chilled glass
